How it started

Mild Hills is a non-profit foundation that aims to provide Ukrainian defenders on the front lines with the necessary protection, ammunition and medical supplies. As russian military has proven themselves to be unscrupulous and non-compliant with the Geneva Convention, Ukrainian soldiers have to be ready for any types of attacks, including those with chemical or even nuclear weapons.

As unbelievable as it gets, this happens in the very centre of Europe in 21st century. Since the very first days of the war, treacherously started by Russia, Ukrainians united under the only common goal – to survive and help each other as much as possible. This provoked an unprecedented wave of grassroots activism and self-organization, and Mild Hills was created as a natural part of this movement. All the expertise on military supply and paramedic needs gained during 8 years of war in the Donbass region made Ukrainians fully prepared for the full-scaled invasion – and such work as the one done by Mild Hills is what could guarantee that as many heroes as possible would be able to celebrate the victory unharmed.

About us

In February 2022, life in Ukraine changed drastically. Putin’s army came to our land, leaving burnt cities and murdered civilians behind. But what Russian invaders didn’t know was that instead of putting up with this situation and obeying to their insane imperialistic ideas, Ukrainians will unite, standing together as one.

Devotion, transparency and unity – these are the values of Ukrainian people that Mild Hills is built on. As the peaceful world we used to know is collapsing, burying thousands of men, women and children under the debris of their own beloved homes, we don’t have time to waste. Instead, we act. Mild Hills is an organisation that works towards making all military supply processes faster, easier and more reliable. Because every bulletproof vest, chemical protection suit or thermal imager delivered on time is not only a saved life of a Ukrainian defender – it is a chance to survive for hundreds of civilians he’s fighting for.
